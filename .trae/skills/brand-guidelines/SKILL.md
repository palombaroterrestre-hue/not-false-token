# Anthropic Brand Styling

## Brand Guidelines

### Colors
- Dark: `#141413`
- Light: `#faf9f5`
- Mid Gray: `#b0aea5`
- Light Gray: `#e8e6dc`
- Orange (Accent): `#d97757`
- Blue (Accent): `#6a9bcc`
- Green (Accent): `#788c5d`

### Typography
- Headings: Poppins (fallback: Arial)
- Body: Lora (fallback: Georgia)
